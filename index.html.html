About Me

I'm a financial services professional with over 25 years of experience in capital markets, specializing in the intersection of traditional finance and emerging digital asset infrastructure.

At SIX Digital Exchange, I led the team that built the world's first fully regulated DLT-based financial market infrastructure. We achieved multiple industry firsts: the first digital bond issuance on a regulated FMI, the first securities settlement using wholesale CBDC in production, and the first interoperability between digital and traditional market infrastructure.

Prior to SDX, I spent over 15 years at State Street, most recently as Global Head of GlobalLink Execution Services, overseeing the Currenex and FX Connect electronic trading platforms. I joined Currenex in 2001 as a founding member of its London operation.

I hold a degree in Theology and Religious Studies from the University of Cambridge. I currently serve on the board of directors at AsiaNext and remain active in advancing institutional adoption of digital assets globally.

Based in Zurich, Switzerland.
